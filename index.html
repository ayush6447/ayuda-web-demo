About Ayuda

Ayuda is a youth-led initiative focused on social impact, innovation, and technology-driven solutions. We empower changemakers, encourage tech-driven solutions, and support social good initiatives. Currently a non-profit, Ayuda aims to build platforms and tools that bring positive societal change using modern technology, while creating a community where volunteers, NGOs, and developers collaborate.
